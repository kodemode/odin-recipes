Creamy Chickpea Curry

This is an incredibly tasty, healthy, and nutritious vegan + gluten-free curry recipe. Packed with plant-based protein, greens, creamy coconut milk and spices - It's bound to be the perfect guilt-free comfort food for a Fall/Winter day! And better yet, it can be quickly made in 30 minutes or under.

♡ ingredients ♡

  • Chickpeas
  • Eggplant
  • Onions and garlic for flavour
  • Curry powder
  • Your choice of spices - Cumin, turmeric, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt & pepper
  • Cooked rice
  • Naan (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro and/or shredded spinach for garnish (optional)

♡ instructions ♡

  1. Cut the eggplant into cubes, and chop the onion into fine & small pieces.
  2. Heat some cooking oil in a pan over medium heat (I recommend avocado oil), before adding the eggplant and onions. Cook them for approximately 10min, until the eggplant becomes tender.
    • Note: You may also make this recipe oil-free by simply replacing oil with water.
  3. Add garlic and drained chickpeas into the mix on the pan.
  4. Proceed by adding coconut milk and all desired spices. Stir well and simmer the curry for about 5min.
  5. In the meantime, make sure to prepare your choice of rice or naan bread.
  6. Once the curry is ready, serve it with rice or naan, and top it all off by adding some fresh cilantro or chopped spinach.

Aaaand done - enjoy! ッ
