Creamy Vegan Curry

♡ Description

This is an incredibly tasty, healthy, and nutritious vegan + gluten-free curry recipe. Packed with plant-based protein, greens, creamy coconut milk and spices - It's bound to be the perfect guilt-free comfort food for a Fall/Winter day! And better yet, it can be quickly made in 30 minutes or under.

♡ Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Eggplant
  • Onions and garlic for flavour
  • Curry powder
  • Spices - cumin, turmeric, smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, salt & pepper
  • Cooked rice of your choice
  • Naan (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro and shredded spinach for garnish (your choice; optional)

♡ Steps

  1. Cut the eggplant into cubes, and chop the onion into fine & small pieces.
  2. Heat some oil or water (depending on whether you wish to make the recipe oil-free) in a pan over medium heat, before adding the eggplant and onions. Cook them for approximately 10min, until the eggplant becomes tender.
  3. Add garlic and drained chickpeas into the mix on the pan.
  4. Proceed by adding coconut milk and all desired spices. Stir well and simmer the curry for about 5min.
  5. In the meantime, cook your choice of rice or naan bread.
  6. Once the curry is ready, serve it with rice or naan, and top it all off by adding some fresh cilantro or chopped spinach. Enjoy :)
