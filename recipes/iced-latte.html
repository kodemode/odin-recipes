Iced Oat Milk Latte

♡ Description

Ever wondered what heaven tastes like? If you're like me and enjoy all things iced or coffee-related, rest assured that you will absolutely love this one. This has been my go-to drink for a few years now - no matter the season - and every day I am so thankful that someone had the genius to invent oat milk (which tastes way better than it sounds!).

This drink is pretty simple and can be made as sweet (or unsweetened) as you'd like, and has the added benefits of being cruelty-free and more sustainable for the planet.

♡ Ingredients

  • 1-2 espresso shots of coffee
  • Oat milk of your choice
    • After trying out many different kinds, I recommend Minor Figures for the best flavour & creaminess
  • Ice cubes
  • Natural sweetener (optional)
    • I recommend maple syrup, but usually prefer this drink unsweetened
  • Reusable straw (optional)
